EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on the 1st day of April, 2025, BETWEEN:

Bharat Innovations Private Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 301, Prestige Tower, MG Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560001 (hereinafter referred to as "the Company"),

AND

Ms. Kavita Sharma, daughter of Mr. Ashok Sharma, residing at 22, Indiranagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560038 (hereinafter referred to as "the Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
   1.1 The Company hereby appoints the Employee as Senior Legal Counsel, and the Employee accepts such appointment.
   1.2 The Employee shall perform such duties and responsibilities as are customarily associated with the position of Senior Legal Counsel, and such other duties as the Company may from time to time reasonably assign.

2. COMMENCEMENT AND PROBATION
   2.1 The Employee's employment shall commence on 1st April, 2025.
   2.2 The Employee shall be on probation for a period of six (6) months from the date of joining. During the probation period, either party may terminate this Agreement by giving fifteen (15) days' written notice.

3. COMPENSATION
   3.1 The Company shall pay the Employee a gross monthly salary of Indian Rupees One Lakh Twenty Thousand (₹1,20,000) per month, subject to applicable deductions.
   3.2 The salary shall be reviewed annually at the discretion of the Company.

4. WORKING HOURS
   The Employee shall work for a minimum of forty-five (45) hours per week, Monday through Saturday, with reasonable flexibility as required by the Company.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   5.1 The Employee shall not, during or after the term of employment, disclose any Confidential Information of the Company to any third party.
   5.2 "Confidential Information" includes all client data, business strategies, trade secrets, and proprietary information of the Company.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   All work product, inventions, developments, and materials created by the Employee in the course of employment shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

7. NON-COMPETE
   For a period of one (1) year following termination of employment, the Employee shall not engage in or be employed by any direct competitor of the Company within the territory of India.

8. TERMINATION
   8.1 After the probation period, either party may terminate this Agreement by giving thirty (30) days' written notice.
   8.2 The Company may terminate this Agreement with immediate effect for cause, including but not limited to gross misconduct, fraud, or breach of this Agreement.

9. GOVERNING LAW
   This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts at Bengaluru.
