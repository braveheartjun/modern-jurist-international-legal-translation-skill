2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

   This Agreement shall remain in force for a period of two (2) years from the date hereof, unless earlier terminated by mutual written consent.

5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
   Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved by arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, with the seat of arbitration at Mumbai.
